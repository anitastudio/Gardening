Learn how to successfully grow herbs in your home garden!

About this event

Herbs are plants which are grown for the special flavor and aroma of their various parts. They have been grown in backyard gardens for centuries and are used to season, enrich, or otherwise improve the taste or smell of certain foods. Learn about those that will grow successfully and when to plant them in the your garden. Presented by Mike Green, Gardener Extraordinaire.
