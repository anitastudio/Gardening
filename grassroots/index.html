Community Grassroots Gardening Movement

A community garden is a collaborative greenspace where the participants share in both the maintenance and the rewards of nature.There is a long tradition of community gardening in various parts of the United States and around the world.

Grassroots Gardener is a non-profit urban gardening program to grow food that is accessible and local right in your yard. Our Outreach Team help others create edible micro gardens in underutilized neighborhood lawn space and provide resources to give you the know how for your home garden.
