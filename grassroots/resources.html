American Horticultural Society
The American Horticultural Society (AHS) is one of the most respected, longstanding national gardening organizations in the country. Since 1922, they have been a trusted source of high quality gardening and horticultural information

Ask EDIS UF/IFAS Extention
Rebranded as “Ask IFAS” in April 2021, UF/IFAS’ Electronic Data Information Source (EDIS) is evolving to meet the needs of their audiences, as well as the faculty who provide research-based, timely information about agriculture, natural resources, family & consumer sciences and youth development.
Gardeners
From solutions for most every gardening challenge to friendly, knowledgeable advice—we're at your service. We design and develop innovative products, manage test gardens, maintain an extensive how-to library, and have a team of down-to-earth gardeners ready to tackle your every question.
