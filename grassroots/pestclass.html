Learn all about insect pests in the home vegetable garden!

About this event

Less than one percent of all insects are harmful. And most of them may be found in and around the home vegetable garden. Who are they, what damage do they cause and how to rid yourself of them will all be presented in this program. Presented by Diane Daisy, Ladybug Fanatic.
